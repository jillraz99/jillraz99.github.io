SITE NAME AND DOMAIN

The site name is Southeast Idaho Happenings and the domain name is seidahohappenings.org. This domain is currently available for $12/year according to Google Domains.

SITE PURPOSE

The goals of Southeast Idaho Happenings are to attract visitors to the small towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven, giving local businesses a platform to advertise, and to inform users about community information such as weather, news and local events, and local artists.

Services provided by this site include:

  • Local weather for each town
  • Major storm watch information for the region
  • Local business list with links to websites
  • Community events
  • Gallery for local artists

These small towns provide ample opportunities to attract visitors who will spend money at local business, thus strengthening the communities and increasing opportunities to its citizens, because with higher revenues comes higher public services and standards of life for all.

Businesses listed for user information include: restaurants, hotels and lodging, adventure outfitters, ATV, RV, and boat rentals, guide services (hiking, kayaking, etc.), museums, state parks, visitor centers, event organizers, local bands and theater companies, golf courses, campgrounds and shops.

Perceived content objectives:

  • Attract out of town visitors to town to spend money at local businesses
  • Encourage local residents and visitors to attend community events
  • Strengthen community pride and spirit
  • Increase local business' profits
  • Give local artists a platform to display their work and gain wider audience and interest
