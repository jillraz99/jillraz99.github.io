COLOR SCHEME

For this color scheme, I chose a pallet of vibrant, earthy tones to echo the adventurous feel of vacationing in Southeastern Idaho. These cool colors communicate freshness and newness, with just a touch of brown to hearken back to the old-time feel of these small, historical towns. These complimentary colors have a strong contrast to make the site interesting and engaging.

These colors are used throughout the site, including in the navigation, headings, and background colors in the header, footer and navigation bar.
