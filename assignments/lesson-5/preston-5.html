Today's Angel

By Rasmus Jilligan

This is a great article about a young boy who showed great courage. On Saturday, April 19, 2020, young Bill Giles came upon a litter of baby kittens. Thinking the mother must be around somewhere, Billy did not think much of it, but when he heard nearby growling coming from the thicket close to the little babies, he knew something must be amiss. With a large stick and nothing but boyhood courage, Billy charged at the thicket yelling at the top of his lungs! A large badger scampered away and Billy quickly took up all the kittens in his jacket and rushed them to safety, before the old badger could change his mind and come back. Three cheers for Billy Giles! If anyone is interested in adopting a kitten, please use the contact button at the bottom of this website to let us know. Billy's mother is proud of her son, but not really interested in owning five new cats!
