Local Farmer's Market

By Rasmus Jilligan

Starting this week the Farmer's Market will bring in local farmers and residents to offer their many varied goods to the public, both locals and visitors. This market will be located in the central plaza square at Main Street and 2nd Street. Market will run weekly on Saturdays until October 2nd and will run from 7am until 2pm.

Expected products for sale will include fresh produce from our local farmers, fresh flowers from local growers, jewerly, quilts, art and other crafts from local residents. Many local business will be showcasing their prominant products. This is a great way to support the community and meet new people. It is also a wonderful opportunity to try new products and for local business to attract new customers!

If you are interested in being a vendor, Please contant Debbie Schultz at 222-2222 to reserve your booth.
