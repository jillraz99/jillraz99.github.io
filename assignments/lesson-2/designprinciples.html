Alignment

Consumer Cellular

Consumer Cellular has done a great job with alignment. The paragraphs have strong left alignment, information on the different options are in boxes that are lined up with each other, and headings are all aligned. The lines on either side of the headers all align and the icons align with each other and the other text on the page.

Repitition

Car Max

This site is a great example of repetition. The same blue and yellow color scheme is found throughout the site. All the butons are bright yellow. The titles are all in the same font and all caps. Paragraphs are in the same font. Icons are all styled with simple line drawings that are very similar in style and size. Everything is center aligned, bringing continuity throughout the site.

Proximity

Just Serve

This site has different sections that are clearly defined with different backgrounds and lots of white space separating them. The paragraphs are tight, so the user can easily see the different sections that belong together. The visual diagram is simply grouped together. The opening page makes it clear where to start and the footer at the bottom has all the icons togehter and lined up.
